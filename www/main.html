About BreastASMiner BreastASMiner is a web-based database that provided a user-friendly resource for explore alternative splicing patterns of molecular type in brest cancer cell lines. Heterogeneity in breast cancer profoundly affects drug responses, treatmnet effectiveness, and clinical outcomes. Alternatie splicing contributes to this variabiliyt by generating distinct RNA isoforms. This resource integrates CCLE's multi-omics data, drug sensitivity profiles, and clinical associations from breast cancer patiets in context of AS events. It also encompasses AS analysis of experimental data based on brest cancer cell liens from the Gene Expression Omnibus.
